About the Game

In tomorrow will be better, you step into the lives of Jim, an aggressive bully shaped by insecurities, and Robert, a quiet victim grappling with isolation. Over the course of a school year, alternate between their perspectives each day, navigating choices that reveal their struggles and strengths. You experience their paths—toward conflict, reconciliation, and tragedy. Can small acts of empathy change their futures?
